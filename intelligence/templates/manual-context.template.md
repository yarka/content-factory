# Intelligence Manual Context

Use this file for optional enrichment that should influence discovery.

## Allowed inputs

- competitor hints
- manual competitor research packs
- analog accounts
- subreddit suggestions
- sites, blogs, newsletters, or channels to watch
- hypotheses about what language or hooks might matter
- audience pain notes from calls, DMs, or workshops
- founder or operator background that should shape discovery
- mission, values, worldview, or team-culture beliefs that should influence interpretation
- profile packaging notes
- featured section breakdowns
- lead magnet and funnel observations

## Annotation format

For each note include:

1. type: evidence / hypothesis / preference
2. source or origin
3. why it matters
4. confidence

## Recommended note types for manual competitor research

- profile links
- screenshots
- notes on positioning
- funnel observations
- featured sections
- resource or lead magnet breakdowns
- what to copy vs what to reject

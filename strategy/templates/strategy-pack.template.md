# Strategy Pack

## Positioning

Write one clear positioning line rooted in the account profile and evidence.

## Audience Lens

Capture who the audience is, what job they are hiring the content to do, and what trust signals they need.

## Competitor Gaps

List where competitors overplay hype, underuse proof, or miss your angle.

## Profile Packaging Recommendations

Recommend a profile headline, banner direction, restrained quantified proof, and CTA framing that fit the account without drifting into guru-style packaging.

## Featured Asset Recommendations

Recommend what should appear in Featured first: walkthrough, audit call, resource, case study, or teardown.

## Funnel and Content Model

Describe how awareness, trust, proof, and soft CTA content should work together on LinkedIn.

## Content Pillars

Define 3-5 pillars with rationale and no-go drift for each.

## CTA Rules

Document what soft CTA styles are allowed and which hard-sell patterns are banned.

## Content Series Map

Map 3-5 recurring series that keep the feed coherent, including where artifact or video proof should appear.

## Adapt vs Reject

List what competitor patterns should be copied, adapted carefully, or rejected because they break authenticity.

## Posting Cadence

Document the recommended weekly cadence and ratio between story, proof, and tactical posts.
